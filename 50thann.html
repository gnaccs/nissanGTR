# Nissan GTR 50th Anniversary Edition

Overview

The Nissan GTR 50th Anniversary Edition celebrates five decades of the iconic GTR lineage, combining cutting-edge technology with a rich heritage.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L V6 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Power: 565 hp
  • Torque: 467 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 196 mph

Features

This special edition includes unique styling cues, a special color palette, and enhanced interior features that pay homage to its storied past.
