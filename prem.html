# Nissan GTR Premium

Overview

The Nissan GTR Premium is a high-performance sports car that combines cutting-edge technology with stunning design. It's known for its powerful engine and exceptional handling, making it a favorite among car enthusiasts.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L V6 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Horsepower: 565 hp
  • Torque: 467 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 196 mph

Features

  • All-Wheel Drive
  • Advanced Aerodynamics
  • Premium Interior with Leather Upholstery
  • High-Performance Braking System
  • Infotainment System with Navigation
