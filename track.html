# Nissan GTR Track Edition

Nissan GTR Track Edition

The Nissan GTR Track Edition is a high-performance sports car that combines cutting-edge technology with thrilling driving dynamics. It's designed for those who crave speed and precision on the track.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L V6 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Horsepower: 565 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 196 mph

Features

  • Advanced Aerodynamics
  • Lightweight Carbon Fiber Components
  • Enhanced Suspension System
  • Track-Optimized Tires
