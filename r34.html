# Nissan GTR R34

Nissan GTR R34

The Nissan GTR R34 is a legendary sports car that has captured the hearts of car enthusiasts around the world. Known for its impressive performance and iconic design, the R34 has become a symbol of automotive excellence.

Specifications

  • Engine: 2.6L RB26DETT Inline-6
  • Power: 276 hp
  • Torque: 368 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 4.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 165 mph

Features

  • All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • Active LSD (Limited Slip Differential)
  • Multi-Function Display
  • Iconic Skyline Design
