# Nissan GTR Nismo

Nissan GTR Nismo

The Nissan GTR Nismo is a high-performance sports car that combines cutting-edge technology with thrilling speed and agility. Known for its powerful twin-turbo V6 engine and advanced aerodynamics, the GTR Nismo is a true icon in the world of performance vehicles.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L V6 Twin-Turbo
  • Horsepower: 600 hp
  • Torque: 481 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 205 mph

Features

  • Advanced All-Wheel Drive System
  • Carbon Fiber Components
  • Upgraded Suspension
  • High-Performance Brakes
