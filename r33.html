# Nissan GTR R33

Nissan GTR R33

The Nissan GTR R33 is a legendary sports car that has captured the hearts of car enthusiasts around the world. Known for its impressive performance and sleek design, the R33 is a true icon in the automotive world.

Specifications

  • Engine: 2.6L RB26DETT Inline-6
  • Power: 276 hp
  • Torque: 368 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 5.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: 156 mph

Features

The R33 comes equipped with advanced features for its time, including:

  • All-wheel drive (AWD)
  • Active LSD (Limited Slip Differential)
  • Multi-link suspension
